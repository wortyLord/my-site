Clan base
A simple concept of a clan base with three floors and a shooting zone, the entrance is made through the second floor from which you can both go up to the third and go down to the first. The house is designed for a small-medium clan due to the small space inside. Taking into account the closed stable bunker, the minimum cost of a raid is 36 rokets, below there will be a detailed analysis of each floor separately

First floor

The house has two symmetrical entrances with air lock, then there is a garage door behind which there is an ascent to the second floor through a hatch, going through the entrance you can find stoves and a locker behind the window openings. also on the first floor there is the main loot, going down through the bunker you will get to the main 2x2. pay attention to the materials so that your version of this house is optimized

Second floor

On this floor you have a room with quick loot points, there is also a lot of space for sleeping bags, beds, mixing tables and much more, in addition there are lockers near the lifts, after going through all the garage doors you will get to the hatch leading to the main

Third floor

Having climbed to this floor you get access to a very large loot room containing at least 20 large boxes, there are also two large batteries, if desired, one of them can be replaced with a TC. This space is designed as an open core.

Fourth floor

Here you can find 4 beds, a couple of lockers and a box. This floor is designed to control the surrounding area. in addition, this floor is protected by turrets and has an exit to the roof.
